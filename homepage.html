Investments

We focus on investing in Asia based technology startups that have the potential to grow regionally and or globally. We believe that the fundamental dynamics underlying the technology industry in developing Asia region offers attractive investment opportunities.

Our Approach

We play an active role in building and growing sustainable ventures. We invest in founders at earliest stages of a startup’s life and partner up to give hands-on advice on product development, market insights and focused execution.

Our Team

Our early stage company building approach is centered on a unique combination of venture building experts and technology experts. We have been building and growing technology companies for many years. Building and managing technology companies runs through the DNA of our organization.
